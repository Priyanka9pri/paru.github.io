About MMTTP

The Malaviya Mission Teacher Training Programme (MMTTP) aims to transform higher education by embedding Indian values and ethos into teaching, research, publications, patents, and institutional development. Aligned with the National Education Policy 2020, the MMTTP focuses on improving the quality of education at all levels by infusing excellence into our educators, students, and the teaching-learning process.

It empowers faculty members to be active participants in institutional and nation-building while fostering their growth as lifelong learners through continuous professional development.
